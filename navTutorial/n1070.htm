Orientation Check Points

You will be given continual practice with specific feedback throughout each lesson. These questions provide you with a way to monitor your progress.

Most questions allow you two opportunities to answer correctly. Feedback will help you understand what was wrong if you chose an incorrect response.

Orientation check point activities are presented in several forms such as matching, multiple choice, and drag and drop. Occasionally, a check point will contain several questions in a drill-and-practice activity.

Be sure to read the instructions on the screen and in the prompt area before attempting to answer an orientation check point question.
