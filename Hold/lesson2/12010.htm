Lesson Overview

In an effort to help you understand the CDC culture, this lesson will discuss the CDC mission, the pledge that all CDC employees take, and the core values to which all CDC employees are expected to adhere. Also in this lesson, you will be given a brief overview of the history of the CDC including highlights of the organization's accomplishments over the past 60 years. Explore this lesson for the information you need to learn about the CDC's mission and values.

Your orientation assignment is to meet the following objectives for this lesson:

  • Define the CDC vision.
  • Define the CDC mission.
  • Define the CDC pledge.
  • Identify the CDC's core values.
  • Recall the history of the CDC.
