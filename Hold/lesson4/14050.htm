Business Partners

 

 

 

 

 

 

CDC partners with a wide range of businesses to facilitate the achievement of public health goals. These partnerships are collaborations in which two or more organizations agree to work together through trust, shared vision, investment, risk, responsibility, evaluation, management, and governance to achieve shared or complementary goals.

CDC partners with businesses to promote health through the products and services businesses offer, the healthcare they purchase, the influence businesses have, and their reach to employees’ families and communities. CDC’s outreach efforts include public health Webinars, conference calls, presentations, and workshops. As a part of their outreach, CDC is also working with businesses to implement communications systems to alert them about critical health issues.
