Public Health Systems

CDC partners with public health systems to improve the development of public health policy and the practice of public health throughout the United States. The organization’s efforts focus on helping strengthen and build the capacity and infrastructure of public health agencies and public health systems.

Through these partnerships, CDC works to develop strategies and facilitate collaborative efforts for achieving CDC’s public health promotion, protection, and preparedness goals. These partnerships also provide the focal point for funding to public health system partners.
