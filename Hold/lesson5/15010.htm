Lesson Overview

Employees at CDC work with a variety of global health partners.  This lesson will discuss the purpose of these partners and their primary functions. Explore this lesson for the information you need to learn about the CDC global health partnerships.

Your orientation assignment is to meet the following objectives for this lesson:

  • Define the purpose of global health partnerships.
  • Identify the CDC’s global health partnerships.
