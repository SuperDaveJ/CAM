Module Summary

You have met your orientation objective for Module 1 and learned about the different CDC culture.

The Department of Health and Human Services is the United States government's principal agency for protecting the health of Americans and providing essential human services.

This module taught you CDC's vision, "Healthy People in a Healthy World-Through Prevention," and mission, to promote health and quality of life by preventing and controlling disease, injury, and disability.

It also identified CDC's core values, which include accountability, respect, and integrity.

CDC seeks to ensure that all people will achieve their optimal lifespan with the best possible quality of health in every stage of life. In addition, CDC seeks to ensure that the places where people live, work, learn, and play will protect and promote their health and safety.

CDC works in close partnership with a wide array of international agencies and institutions to shape global health policies and to fund, implement, and evaluate programs.
