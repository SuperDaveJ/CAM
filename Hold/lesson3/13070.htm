Emerging Health Threats

Another health protection goal of CDC is to ensure that people in all communities will be protected from infectious, occupational, environmental, and terrorist threats.

CDC's preparedness activities—spanning the spectrum from mental health to environmental health—help in safeguarding lives and responding to these threats.  CDC contributes to national, state, and local efforts to prepare for and prevent public health disasters before they occur.

When a disaster has occurred, CDC is prepared to support national, state, and local partners in responding in order to improve public health outcomes.  After response to a disaster has ended, CDC assists these partners in the recovery and restoration of public health functions.
