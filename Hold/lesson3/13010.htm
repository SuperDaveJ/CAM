Lesson Overview

CDC is committed to achieving improvements in people’s lives by accelerating health impacts and reducing health disparities. This lesson will discuss CDC’s health protection goals. It will also discuss the organization’s six strategic imperatives. Explore this lesson for the information you need to become more familiar with CDC’s goals and imperatives.

Your orientation assignment is to meet the following objectives for this lesson:

  • Define CDC’s goals for healthy people.
  • Define CDC’s goals for healthy places.
  • Define CDC’s goals for preparing people for emerging health threats.
  • Define CDC’s goals for a healthy world.
  • Define CDC’s six strategic imperatives.
