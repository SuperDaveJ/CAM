Lesson Overview

Leveraging funding to maximize combat capability is a vital part of the weapon system management process.

In order to manage the provider and customer funds, a working capital fund (WCF) financing mechanism is used to enhance cost awareness and provide requisite flexibility. It’s important to remember that our framework and path forward focuses on aligning financial management with the enterprise-centric process vision established under eLOG21, while streamlining the processes and strengthening accountability for readiness and cost performance.

During this course you’ll see the CAM framework we are using and learn how we can apply the process model to accomplish providing financial data that is consistently reliable, timely, and useful to the USAF commanders and managers across the Service.

Upon completion of this lesson you’ll be able to:

  • Know the importance of the CAM process for WSS.
